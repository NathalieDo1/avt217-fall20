Events

Pines of Nowhere hosts special events in the restaurant. The restaurant allows full buyouts for events but also will allow events hosted on the patio and in the private dining room.

GROUP DINNING

For small Group Dinner Reservations, Small Birthday Events, Suprise Parties, or Other More Casual Special Occasion.

PARTIES

For large Welcoming Receptions, Private Dinners, and Family-Style Dining.

RESERVATIONS

For 4 guests or less
